Our Custom Project Process

1

Detailed Consultation

We start with an in-depth session to understand your unique energy needs, property constraints, and project goals. Our engineers work with you to define the scope, timeline, and budget.

  • Detailed energy load analysis
  • Structural and electrical assessment
  • Review of society or building requirements
  • Budget planning and cost optimization
2

Custom Engineering & Design

Our team designs a custom rooftop solution. We provide detailed system diagrams, 3D renderings, performance simulations, and equipment specifications for your review and approval.

  • System modeling and energy simulation
  • Component selection and compatibility analysis
  • Structural and electrical engineering plans
  • Financial modeling (ROI, LCOE)
3

Project Management & Execution

Once the design is approved, our project managers oversee the entire process. We handle all procurement, logistics, and coordination to ensure a smooth installation.

  • Procurement of all components
  • Professional installation and site management
  • Continuous quality assurance checks
  • Grid interconnection and all necessary approvals
4

Commissioning & Support

After commissioning, we hand over the system and provide comprehensive training. Our post-installation support ensures your system's optimal performance for years to come.

  • Final system handover and documentation
  • Training for society maintenance staff
  • 24/7 remote monitoring setup
  • Scheduled maintenance plans
