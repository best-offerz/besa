Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a rooftop solar system cost?

The cost varies based on your energy needs and roof size. We provide a free, detailed quote after a quick consultation to give you an exact price.

How long does installation take?

A standard residential installation typically takes only 1-2 days. Housing society and commercial projects vary, and we provide a clear timeline upfront.

What happens during a power outage?

Standard grid-tied systems shut down for safety. However, if you add a battery storage system, your home will automatically switch to backup power.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve customers across the nation. Please contact us to confirm service availability in your specific area.
